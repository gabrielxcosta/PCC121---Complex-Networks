# PCC121 – Complex Networks (2025/2)

This repository contains study materials, assignments, and solutions for the course **PCC121 – Complex Networks (2025/2)** at UFOP. The course covers the theory and applications of **graphs and networks**, from basic models to advanced topics such as community detection and network dynamics.

---

## Topics

1. Introduction & Graph Theory
2. Random Networks
3. Scale-Free Property & BA/BB Models
4. Degree Correlation (Assortativity)
5. Robustness & Cascading Failures
6. Community Detection
7. Functional & Dynamical Networks
8. node2vec & GNNs
9. Final Project Seminar

---

* Python, igraph, NetworkX
* Matplotlib, Seaborn, Pandas
